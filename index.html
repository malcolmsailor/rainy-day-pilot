Mal's Rainy Weekend Music Perception Pilot Study

Thank you for participating in this pilot experiment!

You will hear a series of short musical excerpts.

You will hear each excerpt twice.

The first time, you will be asked to rate how sad or happy the music sounded.

The second time, the music will stop abruptly and you will hear a single tone. After hearing the tone, you will be asked how well it fits with the music you just heard.

Please use headphones if possible, and make sure your volume is at a comfortable level.

The pilot should take about 3 minutes to complete. At the end, please click on the button to email me (Malcolm) your results. If you don't click on that button and send the email, I will not get your results! Thanks so so much!
